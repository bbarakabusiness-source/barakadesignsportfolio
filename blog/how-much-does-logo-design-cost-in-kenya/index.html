How Much Does Logo Design Cost in Kenya?

Logo pricing usually depends on the scope, number of concepts, revision rounds, and whether the package includes brand guidelines or other identity assets.

A simple logo concept costs less than a full identity system. If you need strategy, mockups, and multiple applications, the project becomes more involved and should be priced accordingly.

Budget based on the value the logo will bring to your business, not only the design time.

What a Good Package Includes

A strong logo package should include discovery, concept rounds, revision support, and ready-to-use files for web and print.
