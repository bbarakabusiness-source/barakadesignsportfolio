Majestic Maria

Client: Majestic Maria

Role: Designer

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop

Overview

The project centered on creating a visual identity system for Majestic Maria, a fictional five-star hotel in Mombasa, designed to reflect the city’s cultural heritage while communicating luxury, modern comfort, and exceptional service. The identity was designed using a moodboard as a foundation, with emerald green established as the primary brand color to convey luxury and elegance.

Outcome

The outcome was a visually compelling brand identity system that captures attention while conveying a sense of luxury.
