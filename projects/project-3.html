Client: Nomad Nova

Role: Designer

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop

Overview

The design project entailed designing an Instagram carousel for Nomad Nova, a tours and travel company based in Kisumu, Kenya. Being the brand’s debut project, it was essential in defining the brand’s look, tone, and first impression.

Outcome

The outcome was a successful debut tour attended by 23 participants, demonstrating the effectiveness of the carousel in promoting and marketing the event.
