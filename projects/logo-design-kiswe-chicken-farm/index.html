Kiswe Logo Design and Identity System

Client: Kiswe Chicken Farm

Type: Logo Design / Brand Identity

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop

Project Snapshot

This logo design project was created for a Kenyan business looking to build a strong and modern brand identity. The goal was to create a clean, memorable visual system that reflects professionalism and trust.

Overview

The identity system draws inspiration from the shape and natural colors of a rooster, reflecting Kiswe Farm’s commitment to raising healthy chicken.

Outcome

The outcome was a scalable and consistent brand identity system that positions the company for growth beyond a small county farm and into the national market.
