Just Use It

Client: Passion Project

Role: Designer

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop

Overview

The project centered on designing an awareness poster for International Condoms Day aimed at promoting safe sex. Using a banana wearing a condom as the main visual, the design leverages humor and familiarity to capture attention. The slogan “Just Use It” references Nike’s “Just Do It,” while the banana’s tick-like form symbolizes approval, safety, and responsible decision-making.

Outcome

The project resulted in a humorous, attention-grabbing poster that effectively promotes safe sex.
