Good Joseph

Client: Good Joseph

Role: Designer

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects

Overview

I created a logo for the Good Joseph channel, a financial literacy YouTube channel. Because the channel is named after its host, I designed a mascot-style logo to personalize it. I also created a mock-up intro video and other supporting materials. I used a green and gold color scheme, which is strongly associated with financial matters.

Outcome

The project resulted in a bold, recognisable identity system that supports content creation and brand consistency across platforms.
