Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a full brand identity or just a logo?

If your business is new or inconsistent across platforms, a logo alone usually is not enough. A small identity system gives you more consistency and makes marketing easier.

How do I know when it is time to hire a designer?

If your visuals no longer match the quality of your business, or customers keep asking for clearer presentation, that is usually the point where professional design starts paying off.

Can you help with social media and print assets too?

Yes. I design assets for social media, posters, packaging, and other brand touchpoints so the final system works beyond the logo itself.
